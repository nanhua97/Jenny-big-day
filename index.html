18TH BIRTHDAY

Dear Son, my Calvin, Today you turn 18, an important milestone marking your step into adulthood.
我的儿子，凯文：今天你18岁了，恭喜你进入成年人的世界。
Over the past 18 years, we have had the privilege and responsibility of bringing you into this world and supporting you as you grew. We have done our best to provide you with a healthy upbringing and a solid education. From this point on, your life is truly your own.
爸爸妈妈把你带到这个世界，你健康的，快乐的，三观清晰的长到今天，我们作为父母，阶段性任务已经完成。从现在开始，世界在你的脚下。
You will now make your own choices and take responsibility for them. You will shape your own path and learn from your own experiences. We will always be here for you, but we will step back to give you the space to grow independently.
你将亲自掌舵自己的人生，做出自己的选择，也承担相应的责任。爸爸妈妈会始终站在你身后，为你祝福、支持你，但前进的方向，将由你自己决定。
